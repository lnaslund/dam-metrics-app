Total removal cost

Data requirements/sources

The Dam Removal Cost Estimator Tool provides estimates of the total cost of dam removals in the US based on a regression model fit with characteristics and costs of past removals. This model takes as its input:

  • Dam height
  • Dam material
  • Project complexity
  • Annual average discharge
  • Drainage area
  • Region
