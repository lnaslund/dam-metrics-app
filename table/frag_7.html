Connectivity metrics

Data requirements/sources

Connectivity metrics like those described for objectives 5a and 5b can be adapted to represent swiftwater connectivity for recreators.

These metrics can be adapted by accounting for:

  • water quality
  • distance from other recreational amenities
  • flow

Relevant data

Stream map (NHD), dam location data (NID or other database), water quality data (NWIS, state, or local databases), location of recreational amenities (parks and recreation departments, county GIS services)
