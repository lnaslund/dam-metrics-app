Accessible flat water area (km2)

Data requirements/sources

Area of flat water can be discerned from a map of waterbodies (NHD, or other database).

Area metric can be modified to account for water quality and the location of other recreational amenities that affect flat water accessibility (water quality data: NWIS, state, or local databases), location of recreational amenities (parks and recreation departments, county GIS services).
