Impounded sediment volume

Data requirements/sources

Databases such as the Reservoir Sedimentation Database or RESSED and Reservoir Sedimentation Information or RSI.

Can also be estimated using various methods based on variables such as sedimentation rates, sediment producing areas, dam age, reservoir volume, and trapping efficiency.

Alternatively, the probability of sediment impact (Randle and Bountry 2017), calculated as the ratio of reservoir sediment volume to mean annual sediment load, can also be used.

Anchor QEA 2020 produced a cost model for sediment removal which can be used with impounded sediment volume to produce an order of magnitude cost estimate.

Citations

(Minear and Kondolf 2009; Randle and Bountry 2017)
