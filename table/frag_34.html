Submerged hydraulic jumps

Proportion of annual flows classified as dangerous submerged hydraulic jumps (tailwater depth > conjugate depth; discharge lower than the flip point; velocity greater than average swimming speed)

Data requirements/sources

Streamflow data from USGS to calculate flow statistics; spreadsheet from Wahl & Svoboda (2022) to estimate dangerous flows; HEC-RAS models for detailed assessments (may be publicly available for many larger rivers)

Citations

Authors, based on (Wahl & Svoboda 2022)
