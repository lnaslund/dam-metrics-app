Risk of spread

Like other connectivity-based metrics, risk of spread methods vary in biological complexity.

  • Binary indicator of whether an invasive species, disease, or source population for undesirable hybrids is located upstream or downstream of a given dam
  • Invasion risk estimated as the product of the probability of movement through and suitability of a given reach for the invasive species, disease or undesirable source population

Data requirements/sources

Locations of invasive organisms, infected populations, or source population for undesirable hybrids and geospatial model of the river network (e.g., NHDPlus)
