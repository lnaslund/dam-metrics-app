The impact of dam removal on property values is not well-understood. Several hedonic analyses have indicated no impact of small dam removals on property values (Provencher et al. 2008; Cahill & Anguelov 2024), while some have indicated a positive effects (Lewis et al. 2008; Lewis & Landry 2017). The impact of large dam removals on property values is likely greater than that of small removals but empirical evidence is lacking.

Other studies have found a strong perception among riparian residents that dam removal will decrease the value of their lakefront homes (Wyrick et al. 2009), indicating that perceived loss of property value with dam removal would also manifest through riparian resident opposition to dam removal which is a metric outlined for objective 4d) Minimize interruption to community sense of place.
