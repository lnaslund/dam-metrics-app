Upstream number of TRIs, superfund sites, and NPDES discharges

The presence of Toxics Release Inventory sites (TRI), superfund sites, and NPDES discharges indicates the likelihood of reservoir sediment contamination, and hence the need for remediation.

Data requirements/sources

Databases such as the EPA StreamCat Dataset, EPA National Priorities List, EnviroAtlas
