Population size and viability models

Various methods are available to estimate species biomass, population size, or extirpation risk, including those which employ population viability analysis or age-structured population models.

Data requirements/sources

Geospatial model of the river network (e.g., NHDPlus), passability of barriers (see Kemp & O’Hanley 2010), and other required inputs for the population viability analysis or age-structured population model.

Citations

(Corsair et al. 2009; Roy et al. 2018; Ioannidou and O’Hanley 2019; Song et al. 2020)
